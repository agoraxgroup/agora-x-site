## Content Summary

The About Us page tells the story of Agora X, a group of 7 professional women from around the globe who came together in May 2020 during the pandemic to support charitable efforts related to the Mumford & Sons community.

### Team Members
- Carla Kupka
- Francesca Braniger
- Johanne ten Breul
- Judy Kleinschmidt
- Lesya Williams
- Marcy Fox
- Sherry Dibble

### Key Sections
- **Introduction**: Origin story and purpose
- **Guiding Light**: Inspirational mission inspired by Mumford & Sons
- **Mission Statement**: Values-based mission (mutual respect, philanthropy, advocacy)
